Water

Water (chemical formula H2O) is an inorganic, transparent, tasteless, odorless, and nearly colorless chemical substance.

Water can occur in three states:
  1. Solid
    • Ice
    • Snow
    • Frost
  2. Liquid
    • Rain
  3. Gas
    • Clouds
    • Aqueous vapor
